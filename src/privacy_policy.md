# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2, 2026

**PDF Monochrome CCITT G4 Compressor** ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

---

## Summary

**This application processes PDF files entirely on your device. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any servers. All processing happens locally in your browser.**

---

## Data Collection

The App does **NOT** collect any personal data or information from users. Specifically:

- No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- No device identifiers
- No location data
- No usage analytics or statistics
- No cookies or tracking mechanisms
- No crash reports or diagnostics

## Data Processing

All PDF processing occurs entirely on your device:

- PDF files are processed locally in your browser/device memory
- No files are uploaded to any servers
- Processed PDFs remain on your device
- The App operates fully offline after initial download

## Data Sharing

The App does **NOT** share any data with third parties because no data is collected in the first place.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs.

## Open Source

The App is open source software. You can review the complete source code to verify these privacy practices at:

**Repository:** https://github.com/SvobodaJakub/pdf-to-g4-compressor

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above. Given that the App collects no data, changes to this policy are unlikely.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the GitHub repository:

**GitHub Issues:** https://github.com/SvobodaJakub/pdf-to-g4-compressor/issues

## Your Rights

Since the App does not collect, store, or process any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over any files you process with the App, as they never leave your device.
